Overview

Milano air pollution and COVID lockdown

The goal of our project is to produce a visualisation that would help explain the relationship between the pollution measurements and the COVID lockdown starting in March 2020.

Dataset

The dataset we have decided to analyse collects data relating to the measurements of air pollutants in the municipality of Milan. This data is freely available from https://web.comune.milano.it/cortesia/opendata.html. The following pollutants' concentrations are measured: Sign Name NO2 Nitrogen Dioxide CO_8h Carbon monoxide C6H6 Benzene PM10 <10 micrometer particles O3 Ozone PM25 <2.

Explanatory analysis

Genre Our visualization blends elements of the slide show narrative visualization and of the annotated charts. The plots are structured according to a consistent visual style, for example with the color blue identifying the average from the years previous to 2020.
